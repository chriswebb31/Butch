A 2d-adventure set in the late 1800’s. Ladies and gentlemen we introduce you to BUTCH. Butch Cassidy is on a historical trip with a rich story line filled with twist and turns. In a time of cowboys, slavery and The Civil War, Butch must prevail through the creative, interactive maps to complete his missions to unlock the skippable cutscenes, key to the storyline. Talk to the NPC’s (non-player-character) to gain valuable information, loot houses, collect coins and stack up your inventory with your favourite load out and authentic snacks of the era. Gather a crew or go at it alone when standing off against the challenging enemies with varying intelligent abilities. Survive, explore and have fun!
